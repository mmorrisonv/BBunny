The entry period for the 2011 Beach Bunny Model Search begins December 1, 2010, until May 27, 2011, 5:00PM Los Angeles, CA time (the “Entry Period”). Late entries shall be ineligible.

An Entrant must submit her entry form and photographs using the procedures below at any time during the Entry period. To enter the Competition, follow the links at www.beachbunnyswimear.com (the “Web Site”) to the Model Submission page. Entrants must provide all information requested and will be able to upload six (6) recent and different photographs of yourself (must include one headshot and one body shot) in JPEG format (the “Submitted Photographs”), using the instructions provided. All submitted photos must be in color and no other photos will be considered. Only the Entrant (defined below) should appear in the Submitted Photographs. All photos must be at least 500K in size. After completion, your submission will be reviewed and a confirming email will be sent to the address that you provide, with contest information and rules.

The swimsuit is the Semi-Finalists prize to keep. Of the fifteen (15) Semi- Finalists, three (3) Finalists will be selected. One of the finalists will be flown to Los Angeles for a photo/ video shoot by world renowned photographer, Yu Tsai and the chance to appear in Beach Bunny Swimwear’s Catalog. The winner will also receive a $2000 cash prize, as well as $1000 in Beach Bunny Swimwear merchandise.
