Damages

Damage policy applies to 2010 and 2011 Collections only. Beach Bunny’s suits are very delicate and experience normal “wear and tear” over time. Please contact Beach Bunny Swimwear for damages that occur after 60 days for more information.

Care Instructions

Beach Bunny suits are constructed of the finest imported fabrics and trims that are extremely delicate in nature. Many of the suits are similar to fine lingerie and must be cared for in the same manner. While ALL of the suits can be worn in pool or ocean water, they are not intended for strenuous physical water activity or long periods of time in heavily chlorinated water.

All swimwear should be hand washed in cold water with very mild soap (such as specialty lingerie detergent). Lie flat or hang to dry. DO NOT USE WOOLITE!
